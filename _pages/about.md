## About Me
I am Junteng Liu, a first-year PhD candidate at the HKUST NLP Group, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. My core research focus is natural language processing and machine learning.

## Academic Background
- **Ph.D. in Computer Science (2024–Present)** | Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Professor Junxian He (HKUST NLP Group)
- **B.Eng. (2020–2024)** | Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), graduated in June 2024; Recipient of the Zhiyuan Honor Scholarship

## Research Interests
My research spans multiple topics in large language and vision-language models:
1. LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning
2. Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)
3. LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability

## Research Experience
- **Research Intern (February 2025 – Present)** | MINIMAX (AI/technology company)
- **Research Intern (June 2024 – September 2024)** | Tencent WXG; Advised by Zifei Shan
- **Research Intern (June 2023 – December 2023)** | Shanghai AI Lab; Advised by Prof. Yu Cheng

## Skills
- Core research expertise in natural language processing and machine learning
- Hands‑on experience with large language model (LLM) reasoning, truthfulness analysis, and interpretability research
- Specialized knowledge of vision‑language model (VLM) hallucination and chart‑understanding bottleneck problems
- Experience synthesizing verifiable reasoning datasets for LLM training and evaluation
- Proficiency in academic research, experimental design, and peer‑reviewed publication writing
